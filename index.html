About College Room Locator

College Room Locator is a comprehensive platform designed to help students, faculty, and staff navigate their college life with ease. Our platform provides accurate and up-to-date information about teachers, nearby shops, and classroom details, making it easier for you to focus on what matters most.

Here are some of the key features of College Room Locator:

  • Find Teachers: Access detailed information about your teachers, including their subjects, contact details, and office hours.
  • Discover Nearby Shops: Locate shops, marts, and other facilities around the campus to meet your daily needs.
  • Classroom Details: Get information about classrooms, including their locations, capacities, and available equipment.
  • Easy Search: Use the search bar to quickly find information by name, email, or department.

Whether you're a new student or a returning one, College Room Locator is here to make your college experience smoother and more enjoyable.
